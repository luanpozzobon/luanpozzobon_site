MoonVs

The MoonVs project, is an Application that consumes the TMDB API, with this service is possible to retrieve data information about Movies and TV Shows, such as genres, ratings and where to watch. It is also possible to create profiles and make your own rate of each Movie and TV Show. It's one of my first projetcs, and probably the best so far.
